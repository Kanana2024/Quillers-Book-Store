Our History

Founded in 1995 in Nairobi, Quillers's Bookstore started as a small, family-run shop with a deep love for African stories. Over the past three decades, we've grown into one of the region's most trusted names in books—home to thousands of titles across every genre imaginable.

Our mission has always been simple: to connect readers with stories that inspire, educate, and transform. From our modest beginnings to our global presence today, we remain devoted to celebrating literature and the power of imagination.

Today, our dedicated team of works tirelessly across our stores and digital platforms to bring readers closer to more books and many authors worldwide. At Quiller's, we believe every story deserves to be shared—and every reader deserves to find theirs.
